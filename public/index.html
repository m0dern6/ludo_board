Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 1, 2024

This Privacy Policy applies to the **Ludo Board: Classic Ludo Game** application ("App") for mobile devices. The App is provided at no cost and is intended for use as-is.

1. Information Collection and Use

This App does not require you to provide any personally identifiable information. We do not require you to create an account, nor do we collect, store, or share your personal data such as name, email address, or phone number.

The game operates entirely offline. All game preferences (such as custom rules and audio settings) are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to any external servers.

2. Third-Party Services and Analytics

Our App currently does not employ third-party analytics or tracking tools. If we decide to use third-party services in the future (such as advertising networks or crash reporting tools like Firebase Analytics), this policy will be updated immediately, and any data collected will be anonymized and strictly used to improve the app.

3. Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal data, your information is safe by default. The App only accesses the internal storage necessary to save your local game state and settings.

4. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Given that the App operates offline and collects no data, it is completely safe for families and children of all ages to play.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us.
