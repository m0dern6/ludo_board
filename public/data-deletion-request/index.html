User Data Deletion Request

Submit this form and your default mail app will open with the request prefilled for info@zenithdigitalsolution.com.np.

This will open your email app with the request ready to send.

Please complete all required fields before submitting.

What To Include

  • Your full name and contact email.
  • Your in-app account email if it is different.
  • A clear description of the data you want deleted.

What Happens Next

After submission, your mail app opens with a completed message addressed to info@zenithdigitalsolution.com.np.
